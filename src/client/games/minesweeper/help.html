Minesweeper

Description

Sweep all the mines within the board.

Rules

  • Reveal the tiles reveal whether its a number or a mine.
  • You can open the first tile at random and there will never be a mine.
  • The number is the total mines adjacent to the tile.
  • Place a flag when you're sure there's a mine in it, otherwise it's safe to open it..
  • Mark all the mines with flags and reveal all the remaining tiles to win the game.

Controls

  • Left Click: Reveal tile
  • Right Click: Toggle flag
  • Double Click: Reveal surrounding tiles
